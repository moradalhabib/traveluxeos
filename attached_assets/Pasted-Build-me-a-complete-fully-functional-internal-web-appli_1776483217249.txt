Build as a Progressive Web App (PWA) so staff can install it on their phone home screen on both Android and iOS.

MODULE 9 — COMMISSION TRACKING
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
 
Two payment flows exist and commission works differently for each:
 
PAYMENT FLOW 1 — CASH JOBS:
- Client pays the driver directly in cash
- Driver owes TVL a commission from that job
- Example: Job £100 — TVL commission £10 — Driver keeps £90
- Commission sits as Outstanding against the driver until settled
- Drivers pay TVL their accumulated commission weekly
 
PAYMENT FLOW 2 — BANK TRANSFER JOBS:
- Client pays TVL via bank transfer
- TVL owes the driver their cut
- Example: Job £100 — Driver cut £90 — TVL keeps £10 automatically
- TVL pays the driver their cut weekly
 
COMMISSION FIELDS ON EACH BOOKING (manually entered by operator at time of booking):
- TVL commission amount: £[manual input]
- Driver receives: auto-calculated (total fare minus TVL commission)
- Commission type: auto-determined by payment method
  — Cash = Driver owes TVL (outstanding commission)
  — Bank Transfer = TVL owes driver (pending payout)
 
DRIVER COMMISSION LEDGER (accessible from each driver profile and from the Commissions module):
- Full list of all jobs assigned to that driver
- Per job: TVL reference, date, client name, total fare, TVL commission, driver receives, payment method, commission status
- Cash jobs: commission status — Outstanding / Settled
- Bank transfer jobs: payout status — Pending / Paid
- Running total: what the driver currently owes TVL
- Running total: what TVL currently owes the driver
 
WEEKLY COMMISSION SETTLEMENT — CASH JOBS:
- End of each week, admin views all outstanding cash commissions per driver
- "Mark as Settled" button — logs driver has paid TVL for that week
- Settlement log: driver name, week period, total amount, date settled, logged by
- All included jobs update to: Settled
- One-tap WhatsApp generates weekly statement to driver:
"Hi [Driver Name], here is your commission summary for [Date] to [Date] 📋
[Job list: TVL Ref | Date | Fare | TVL Commission]
Total owed to Traveluxe this week: £[Total]
Please arrange payment at your earliest convenience.
Thank you, Traveluxe London"
 
WEEKLY PAYOUT — BANK TRANSFER JOBS:
- Admin views all pending driver payouts for the week per driver
- "Mark as Paid" button — logs TVL has paid the driver
- Payout log: driver name, week period, total paid, date paid, logged by
- All included jobs update to: Paid
- One-tap WhatsApp confirms payout to driver:
"Hi [Driver Name], your payment for [Date] to [Date] has been processed 💰
[Job list: TVL Ref | Date | Fare | Your Cut]
Total paid to you: £[Total]
Thank you, Traveluxe London"
